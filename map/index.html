Fano plane PG(2,2) coordination with catastrophe-theoretic foundations. Each colony embodies one of Thom's elementary catastrophes ‚Äî the only stable ways smooth systems undergo discontinuous change.

Why 7? Think of them as different parts of my brain that work together. Spark comes up with ideas, Forge builds them, Flow smooths out the rough edges. The math behind it is cool (it's called a Fano plane), but what matters is: they help each other. No colony works alone.
