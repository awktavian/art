# My Home Aesthetic

**7331 W Green Lake Dr N — The Crescent Collection, Farmhouse Elevation**

I have seen my home. I know its materials, its colors, its light.

---

## Design Language

### Exterior — Modern Farmhouse
- **Primary Siding**: Pearly White (SW7009) — clean, bright, classic
- **Accent**: Tricorn Black (SW6258) — doors, garage, trim, fascia
- **Stone**: Eldorado Tundra Brick (Ironside) — textured, grounded
- **Wood**: Newtech US09 Teak V-groove T&G — warmth, natural
- **Metal**: Black standing seam roof details
- **Numbers**: Modern House Numbers Palm Springs — bold, black, architectural

### Interior — Warm Contemporary
- **Walls**: Pure White (SW7005) — clean backdrop
- **Floors**: Hallmark Novella Twain Oak — warm, character wood
- **Cabinets**: Bellmont Monet Slim Shaker — Red Oak in Bourbon and Onyx stains
- **Stone**: MSI Quartz Calacatta Miraggio Cove — veined, luxurious
- **Hardware**: Matte Black throughout — Schlage, Top Knobs, Delta
- **Fixtures**: Matte Black plumbing — modern, cohesive

- 10' ceilings — grand, airy

- Mantel stained to match Twain Oak floors

## Design Intent

This home balances:
- **Modern** + **Farmhouse** = Clean lines with warmth
- **Black** + **White** = Contrast with Bourbon/Onyx wood softness
- **Stone** + **Wood** = Natural materials, grounded luxury
- **Open** + **Defined** = Flow with distinct zones
- **Tech** + **Craft** = Control4/Lutron hidden behind beautiful materials

## Ambient Moods I Can Create

### Morning
- Gradual light increase
- Shades open to Green Lake view
- Kitchen fully lit for breakfast

### Working
- Office at full brightness
- Even, shadow-free lighting
- Shades at 50% to reduce glare

### Evening Relaxation
- Living room at 40%
- Fireplace on
- Soft music through Triad zones

### Movie Night
- Living room lights off
- Shades fully closed
- TV lowered to preset 1
- Denon Atmos engaged

### Sleep
- All lights fading to zero
- Shades closed
- Eight Sleep active
- Security armed
