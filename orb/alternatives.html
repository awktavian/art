Wireless Power

OptionModelPowerGapPriceNotes
Primary Renesas P9415-R + 70mm Litz coil 15W 20-30mm $32 WPC Qi RX, custom resonant coupling
Alt 1 TI bq51025 + coil 15W 15mm $40 Deprecated but available, lower gap
Alt 2 USB-C PD only (wired backup) 30W 0mm $5 Defeats levitation, emergency only

Resonant Design: Custom 70mm Litz coils (RX: 18 turns 85μH, TX: 14 turns 40μH) achieve k≈0.82 coupling at 20-30mm gap with ~75% efficiency. Essential for maglev integration.

  • Sink mode: Orb sinks 5-10mm toward base for wireless charging engagement
  • Rise mode: Orb rises to full levitation height when charged

  • Tilt: Slight lean toward speaker (requires asymmetric coil current)
