The Complete Taylor Universe Update

It's been 10 years since Tool Time signed off. Create a comprehensive "Where Are They Now?" feature covering all major characters - their careers, relationships, families, and how they've grown (or not grown) since we last saw them.

Home Improvement finale aired May 25, 1999. Your "Where Are They Now?" is set in 2009.

The Assignment

Entertainment magazines love reunion features. Your task: write a comprehensive "Where Are They Now?" article that catches up with the entire Home Improvement cast of characters. This isn't just a list - it's narrative journalism that tells the continuing story of people we came to love.

Think about who these characters were, what they valued, and where they were headed. Then project forward 10 years. What choices would they have made? What surprises would life have thrown at them? How would they have changed - or stubbornly stayed the same?

Requirements

  • Magazine Article Format: Introduction, individual profiles, connecting narrative
  • All 8 Characters: Each character needs at least 75-100 words of update
  • Career Updates: What is each person doing professionally?
  • Relationship Status: Married? Kids? Divorced? Still dating?

  • Surprises: At least 2-3 unexpected but believable developments
  • Interconnections: How do these lives still intersect? Who stayed close?
