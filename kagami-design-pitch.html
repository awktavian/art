kagami/philosophy

Three Principles

Not screens we look at, but worlds we inhabit.

01

World > UI

The background is not decoration—it's the world. Elements live within it. Interface floats above. In spatial computing, this becomes literal.

02

Subtle > Dramatic

Restraint is sophistication. A 4px displacement beats 20px. On wearables, where attention is precious, subtlety becomes essential.

03

Sense → Influence → Persist

Every interaction: detect presence, subtly modify the world, let changes linger. The user doesn't just touch—they influence.
