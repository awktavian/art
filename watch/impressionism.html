Impressionism

1860s — 1880s

The Impressionists revolutionized painting by capturing the fleeting effects of light and atmosphere. Working en plein air, artists like Monet, Renoir, and Degas used visible brushstrokes and vibrant colors to convey momentary impressions. This watch evokes their garden scenes with soft pastels, dappled light effects, and a water lily motif.
