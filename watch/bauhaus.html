Bauhaus

1919 — 1933

Founded by Walter Gropius in Weimar, Germany, the Bauhaus school unified art, craft, and technology. Its philosophy—"form follows function"—revolutionized design. This watch embodies core Bauhaus principles: primary colors at cardinal positions, each assigned a geometric form (triangle, circle, square), with clean sans-serif typography throughout.
