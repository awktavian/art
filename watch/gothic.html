Gothic

12th — 16th Century

Gothic architecture soared toward the heavens with pointed arches, ribbed vaults, and luminous stained glass. Cathedrals like Notre-Dame and Chartres became canvases of light filtering through jewel-toned windows. This watch evokes the rose window's radial symmetry, the warmth of candlelit gold, and the verticality that defined medieval Europe's sacred spaces.
