Art Deco

1920s — 1940s

Art Deco emerged in Paris before World War I, embracing luxury, glamour, and faith in technological progress. Characterized by bold geometric forms, rich colors, and lavish ornamentation, it drew inspiration from Cubism, the Ballets Russes, and ancient Egyptian art. This watch interprets the movement's signature sunburst motifs and stepped chrome detailing.
