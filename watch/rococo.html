Versailles

Rococo

Early — Late 18th Century

Rococo emerged in France as a reaction to Baroque grandeur, favoring intimacy, elegance, and playful decoration. Antoine Watteau and François Boucher painted fêtes galantes in soft pastels, while interiors bloomed with shell motifs and asymmetrical curves. This watch captures the era's feminine grace—powdered pink, celestial blue, and gilded flourishes.
