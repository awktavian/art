Surrealism

1920s — 1950s

Surrealism sought to unlock the creative potential of the unconscious mind. Salvador Dalí's melting clocks in "The Persistence of Memory" and René Magritte's impossible juxtapositions challenged perceptions of reality. This watch combines the desert dreamscapes, long shadows, and iconic imagery that defined the movement's exploration of time and consciousness.
