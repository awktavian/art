De Stijl

1917 — 1931

Piet Mondrian and Theo van Doesburg founded De Stijl ("The Style") in the Netherlands, reducing art to its essential elements: primary colors, black, white, and orthogonal lines. They sought universal harmony through pure abstraction. This watch translates Mondrian's iconic grid compositions into a functional timepiece.
