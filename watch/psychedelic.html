Psychedelic

1965 — 1972

Psychedelic art emerged from the 1960s counterculture, visualizing altered states of consciousness. Wes Wilson, Victor Moscoso, and Peter Max created swirling, saturated imagery for concert posters and album covers. Inspired by Art Nouveau curves and optical effects, this watch pulses with neon rainbows and spiraling color—time as an endless, expanding trip.
