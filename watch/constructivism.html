Chronos

Constructivism

1915 — 1940s

Born in revolutionary Russia, Constructivism rejected "art for art's sake" in favor of design serving society. Vladimir Tatlin, Alexander Rodchenko, and El Lissitzky created bold compositions using geometric forms and industrial materials. This watch embodies the movement's signature red-black-cream palette and dynamic angular composition.
