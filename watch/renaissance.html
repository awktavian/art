Rinascimento

Renaissance

14th — 17th Century

The Renaissance marked the rebirth of classical ideals in art, architecture, and learning. Leonardo da Vinci, Michelangelo, and Raphael pursued mathematical harmony and naturalistic beauty. This watch embodies the period's golden proportions, sfumato gradients, and the rich warmth of Florentine ateliers where art and science intertwined.
