Floreal

Art Nouveau

1890 — 1910

Art Nouveau artists like Alphonse Mucha and Gustav Klimt drew inspiration from organic forms—flowing hair, twisting vines, and the wings of dragonflies. The movement sought to break down the distinction between fine and applied arts. This watch channels its sinuous curves, botanical motifs, and the warm metallic tones of Tiffany glass.
