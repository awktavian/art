Pop Art

1950s — 1970s

Pop Art emerged from British and American artists who drew on popular culture—advertising, comic books, and consumer products. Andy Warhol, Roy Lichtenstein, and Jasper Johns blurred the boundaries between "high" and "low" art. This watch celebrates the movement's bold outlines, Ben-Day dots, primary colors, and comic book energy.
