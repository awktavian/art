Cubism

1907 — 1920s

Pablo Picasso and Georges Braque shattered traditional perspective by depicting subjects from multiple viewpoints simultaneously. Analytical Cubism fractured forms into geometric planes in muted earth tones, while Synthetic Cubism introduced collage elements. This watch fragments time itself across overlapping facets and displaced numerals.
